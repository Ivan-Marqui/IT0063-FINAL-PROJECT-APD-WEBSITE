Mission

Empower and Encourage Excellence: Provide a platform for students to showcase their talents, skills, and abilities.

Foster Collaboration and Inclusivity: Cultivate an inclusive and welcoming environment that fosters collaboration, camaraderie, and mutual support among students from diverse disciplines. We aim to build lasting friendships and professional networks.

Advocate for Students Interests: Act as a representative body for the student community at FEU Diliman, addressing their needs, concerns, and aspirations. We strive to advocate for their rights, well-being, and development within the academic institution.

Promote Responsible and Ethical Use of Technology: Promote ethical values and practices among our members, emphasizing responsible and sustainable use of technology. We aim to raise awareness about the impact of technology on society and encourage responsible digital citizenship.

Engage with Industry and Society: Facilitate interactions and partnerships with industry professionals, technology enthusiast, and other student organizations to enrich the knowledge and perspectives of our members. We actively contribute to community development through interdisciplinary and technology-based initiatives.

Vision

Our vision is to foster a vibrant and inclusive community where all college students at FEU Diliman can flourish. We aspire to be a leading organization that empowers and supports students from diverse disciplines in showcasing their skills, fostering creativity, and excelling in both scholastic and non-scholastic pursuits. Through collaboration and innovation, we aim to drive personal growth, professional development, and positive change across the campus and in the broader field of information technology.

APD's History

APD stands for the Assemblage of Programmers and Developers. It is the sole IT academic organization at FEU Diliman, established on the 8th of August 2022 by its founder and former president, Jabez Chzammuth Tumbaga. APD is dedicated to fostering a community of students passionate about IT.
